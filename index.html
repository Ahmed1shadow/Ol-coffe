espresso

Heavy black Italian coffee that has a bitter taste as no sugar is added to it. It is prepared by boiling the coffee with a little water,and it can be diluted with an additional amount of water, milk or vanilla flavor

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is one of the most common types of coffee. Cappuccino is prepared by mixing equal quantities of espresso coffee with concentrated milk, and it is possible to add many flavors to it, such as the flavor of hazelnut, almond and vanilla, and what distinguishes the cappuccino is to put a layer of cream and foam on it

Arabic coffee

It is considered one of the oldest types of coffee, and it is prepared from well-roast coffeed coffee beans, and it is ground and boiled with cardamom and saffron, and it is served once without sugar, in special small cups, and it is one of the origins of hospitality in most Arab countries
